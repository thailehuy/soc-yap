Strengths

  • Rage Outburst-3 AT 4* gives 70% magic attack conversion and armor piercing at 5*
  • Trait + Reaction + Wrath Debuff offers solid self-sufficiency
  • Bosses with large hitboxes take extra damage from AoE
  • Ascension makes Hassle usable in rotation with lower CDs and more rage
  • Short legs (little movement) — pairs well with Homa

Weaknesses

  • Cannot sustain Act Again due to limited actions post armband
  • Main DPS skills on 3CDs reduce cycle efficiency
  • Ascension skill does not address core flaws

Skills

  • Rank 11 - Wrath Devastation: AoE option with stun
  • Rank 9 - Charged Attack: Fills downtime; no NRG gain from other basic
  • Rank 7 - Atk Command: Team ATK +12% buff
  • Rank 5 - Strength Activation / Contempt (Castelia): Prefer Strength for sustain unless DMG2 source exists
  • Rank 3 - Warm Touch / Hassle (Castelia): Hassle gives Rage; Warm Touch activates reaction skill
  • Rank 1 - Knightly Spirit: Preferable over Crumbling Smash

Loadout

  • General: A comfy general set
  • Post 2-Star, Pre 4-Star: Main pre-4-star loadout
  • With External DMG Buff: Run general with Contempt
  • Boss: Max DPS + minor ATK% support
